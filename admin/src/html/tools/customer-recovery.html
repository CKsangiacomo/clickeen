Internal control

Customer Recovery

Targeted company-plane actions for locked-out customer recovery and abusive non-owner member removal.

This tool is intentionally narrow. It does not browse accounts or switch DevStudio into a customer shell.

Recover customer email

Update auth plus canonical profile email after manual verification. The new address is left unverified. 


          


          

            

              
Remove non-owner member

              

                Remove a viewer, editor, or admin from the target account. Owner removal stays out of scope and must use the dedicated ownership path.
              

            


            

              
              
              
              
            


            

            

          


          

            

              
Revoke user sessions

              

                Revoke all active Berlin sessions for the target user after recovery or abuse response. This is scoped to a concrete account/user pair and recorded in the audit log.
